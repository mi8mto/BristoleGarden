ABOUT US

Bristole Garden is one of the country’s most celebrated restaurants; the creation of leading British restaurant group.

The restaurant is an organic space reflective of nature inspired cuisine. The interplay of trees and colour brings life and a vibrance that embraces the restaurant’s place.

An ode to the British landscape, from the vast sky floor, to the cracked bark of a paperbark tree, every detail from the ground up has been thoughtfully considered.

OUR TEAM

Bristol Garden supported by a longstanding team of passionate executives and staff.

The new incarnation of Bristol Garden brings an exciting change; a chance for the team to create a more personal dining experience for our guests, underpinned by a generous dose of humble hospitality and a sense of understated luxury.

COVID-19

The safety of our guests and staff is our top priority, and therefore we are taking the following COVID-19 precautionary measures to ensure a safe and comfortable dining experience for all.

  • All staff have provided proof of vaccination.
  • We have installed a Needlepoint BiPolar Ionization air circulation system which tested and proven effective in independent laboratory tests against COVID-19 particles.
  • Hand sanitizer stations are onsite & available to all guests.
  • We have implemented a strict cleaning and sanitizing regimen, including disinfecting our restrooms every 30 minutes.
